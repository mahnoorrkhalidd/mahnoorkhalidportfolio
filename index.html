    Logo Brand Design

    For this project, I designed a logo for Coffee Palace, a cozy and modern coffee shop. The objective was to create a logo that reflects the warm and inviting atmosphere of the café while maintaining a sleek and contemporary design.

    The logo features a stylized coffee cup with steam rising from it, symbolizing the fresh and aromatic coffee served at Coffee Palace. The typography is clean and elegant, with the word "Coffee" taking center stage and "Palace" subtly nestled below it. The circular design element encapsulates the logo, giving it a cohesive and polished look.
